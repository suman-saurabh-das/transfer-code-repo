[01] gRPC

  It is developed by google and is open source.
  gRPC works on RPC (Remote procedure call, which enables us to call functions defined in remote server from the client, client can be another server, it doesn't have to be a browser).
  It is used to have effective communication between 2 systems.
  It can be used for load balancing, tracing, health checking, authenticating, etc.
